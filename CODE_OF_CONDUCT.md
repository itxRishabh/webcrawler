# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful and inclusive
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, trolling, or insulting comments
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Using this software for illegal activities

## Responsible Use

Contributors must ensure that:
1. All contributions comply with applicable laws
2. No code is contributed that facilitates illegal use
3. The tool remains intended for legitimate archival purposes only

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or illegal behavior may be reported to the project maintainers.
